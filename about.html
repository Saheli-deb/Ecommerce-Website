Institute of Engineering and Management

IEM (Institute of Engineering and Management) is a prestigious educational institution in India known for offering quality education in engineering, management, and related fields. Here's an overview:

  • General Information
      • 1. Location: IEM is located in Kolkata, West Bengal.
      2. Affiliation: The institute is affiliated with University of Engineering and Management (UEM), Kolkata
      3. Establishment: Founded in 1989, IEM is one of the oldest private engineering colleges in the region.
  • Programs Offered
      • 1. Undergraduate:
        - B.Tech in disciplines like Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics & Communication, Electrical, and more.
        - BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration).
      2. Postgraduate:
        - M.Tech in various engineering streams.
        - MBA (Master of Business Administration).
        - MCA (Master of Computer Applications).
      3. Other Programs:
        - Integrated courses and specialized programs in data science, artificial intelligence, and related fields. - Integrated courses and specialized programs in data science, artificial intelligence, and related fields.
  • Features
      • 1. Infrastructure: Modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, a central library, and research facilities.
      2. Placements: IEM has a strong placement record with top companies like TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Amazon, and Capgemini recruiting students.
      3. Industry Interaction: Regular industry visits, seminars, and workshops to enhance practical knowledge.
      4. Ranking: Consistently ranked among the top private engineering and management colleges in India.
  • Extracurriculars
      • 1. Cultural Events: Annual fests like *IEM-MCA Fest* and other cultural activities.
      2. Technical Events: Hackathons, coding contests, and project exhibitions.
      3. Clubs and Societies: Student-led clubs for robotics, music, drama, and entrepreneurship.
